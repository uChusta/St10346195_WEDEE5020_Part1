About Us

Established in December 2024, CloudTech Inc. was created to provide the latest and greatest the computer and gaming world has to offer. Our Goal is to provide quality computer parts and affordable computer repairs for your gaming, office or personal needs. Our services are available to any and all computer users be it office, gaming or personal computers.

Our Mission

At CloudTech Inc., our mission is to deliver top-notch computer repair services and high-quality computer parts to our customers. Along with our wide range of computer parts, we also stock a variety of video game accessories both online and in-store. We strive to ensure that every client receives personalized attention and solutions tailored to their specific needs.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading provider of computer repair services and parts in the industry. We aim to build long-lasting relationships with our customers based on trust, reliability, and quality service.

We are committed to providing excellent customer service and support. If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us.
